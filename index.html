About us

Data-driven Solutions for Education At uPlanner, we dedicate ourselves to the development and implementation of data-driven solutions for higher education.

Misión

To promote efficient management in educational institutions through data-driven solutions that impact every member of the academic community and generate a substantial contribution to the formation of the professionals of the future.

Visión

To be the EdTech company with the greatest global impact on education through data-driven solutions.

Values

We believe in the power of technology to positively impact education in order to transform people and societies. We are dedicated and passionate about what we do. We are committed to providing our clients with excellent service, results and transparency, aligned with their goals and objectives, in order to create long-term relationships.

We believe that teamwork and collaboration are essential for ideas to be strengthened and turned into successful actions with concrete results. We promote the creation of relationships with clients, allies and collaborators, which favor an environment of integrity, characterized by commitment and constant support in order to achieve medium and long term objectives.

Innovation is our main driving force. We work to become the best version of ourselves. To this end, we are always promoting lifelong learning for our organization, our professionals, our clients and our partners.
